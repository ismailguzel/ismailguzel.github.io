About me

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics as the Valedictorian from the Dokuz Eylul University in Izmir, Turkey. I then received my M.Sc. degree from the same university. I am currently working towards my Ph.D. degree in mathematics engineering at ITU in Istanbul, Turkey. with supervisor Prof. Dr. Atabey Kaygun. Between August 2021 and August 2022, I am a visiting researcher at computational mathematics, science and engineering, Michigan State University with supervisor Asst. Prof. Dr. Elizabeth Munch.
