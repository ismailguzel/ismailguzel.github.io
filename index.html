About me

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in the department of mathematics (major) as the Valedictorian and the deparment of statistics (minor) from the Dokuz Eylül University in İzmir, Türkiye. I then received my M.Sc. degree from the same university. I am currently working towards my Ph.D. degree in the department of mathematics engineering at İTÜ in İstanbul, Türkiye. Between August 2021 and 2022, I am a visiting researcher at computational mathematics, science and engineering, Michigan State University, USA with supervisor Dr. Elizabeth Munch thanks to TÜBİTAK 2214-A International Doctoral Research Fellowship Programme.
